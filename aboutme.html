~ about me ~

Hi, my name is Christian, and I am a seventeen-year-old highschool student from Cohasset, Massachusetts. I have lived most of my life in this town, having moved away from Lake Forest, Illinois, at the age of five. Some interests of mine include technology, coding, coastal studies, and rowing. My jobs reflect these passions of mine, as I have worked as both a coxswain and shore manager at a local rowing club for the past two years, helping others experience the joys I have found in the sport.

An image published by the Gloucester Times, showing me wearing the #2 jersey. Here, I am about to set out on a 20-mile circumnavigation of Cape Ann, the longest race I have yet to row.

Another thing I have been involved with that reflects my interests is the Cohasset Center for Student Coastal Research, where I have also been involved throughout my highschool experience. Over this past summer, I spent much of my time developing an autonomous drone to take aerial scans of coastal regions, a project that combined several of my hobbies.

This is a film produced my a friend of mine, chronicling some of my work done at CSCR.

Outside of rowing, I am also involved in my school's tennis team, where I play for varsity throughout the spring. Musically, I feel that I enjoy all genres, though you can find out more about my tastes at my music blog! Here, I talk about my favorite albums, go into depth about their histories and legacies, and give my rankings and opinions of my favorite songs. I have two younger sisters, Schuyler and Phoebe, and live with both of my parents and my maternal grandmother. I am hoping to get into a good college over this coming school year, and hopefully enroll next fall!

Also, if you would like to know a bit more about my hobbies and passions, I have created a fanpage for the game series LittleBigPlanet, a personal favorite of mine. Hopefully if you read this page through, you will want to try it out as well!
