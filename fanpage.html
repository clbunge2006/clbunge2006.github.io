One of my favorite franchises, LittleBigPlanet is a game series exclusive to the PlayStation, with three main entries to the franchise. These games hold a special place for me, as they were a cornerstone of my early childhood. When I was young, my grandparents owned a PS3, and were the only ones in my family that had this game. Thus, wherever I think of LittleBigPlanet, I am reminded of those trips to Maine long ago.

A promotional screenshot from the first LittleBigPlanet game.

The series has survived several generations of consoles, with the three main installments being playable on the PS3 through the PS5. Published by Sony, this game has passed through myriad game developers, having moved on to its eleventh studio by 2023. Indeed, such a varied development schedule has led to various problems with the game's long-term health. Though the game's tagline is “Play, Create, Share,” discontinuation of the game's official servers has diminished the game's status.

A photo of LittleBigPlanet Karting, a spinoff game which took the creative concepts of LittleBigPlanet and applied them to a karting platform. As a kid, I spent hours creating tracks for my siblings and I to race on, leading this game to hold a special place in my heart as well.

During its prime, sharing and playing user-generated content was a mainstay of the LittleBigPlanet experience, with large parts of the community and content for the game being destroyed by Sony's decision to pull all official servers offline. Indeed, since the release of the third and final installment to the official series, LittleBigPlanet 3, the series has entered into a long state of steady decline. By October 31st of this year, the final official installment to the series, LittleBigPlanet 3, will be delisted from the PS4 marketplace, along with all its DLC.

LittleBigPlanet 3, the final installment to the series, was removed from the PlayStation Store on October 31st, 2024.

As this game holds such sentimental value for me, I am disheartened by Sony killing all official support for this game, despite the fans who were still very much invested in it. I hope that sometime in the future, someone else who is passionate about the game gets a chance to revive it, giving back to the community that supports and cherishes these games.
